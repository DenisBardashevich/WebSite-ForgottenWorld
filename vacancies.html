Comprehensive Training Program

We provide detailed training sessions for all new team members, ensuring they are well-prepared for their roles. From learning customer service skills to mastering game mechanics, our structured training covers all essential aspects of working in an immersive quest environment.

Our experienced trainers guide new hires through real-world scenarios, offering hands-on experience before they interact with actual players. This ensures that every employee feels confident and equipped to deliver outstanding experiences.

Employee Benefits

Enjoy competitive salaries, flexible schedules, and an exciting work environment full of challenges. At Forgotten World, we prioritize the well-being of our employees and offer a range of perks designed to support both personal and professional growth.

Some of our benefits include health insurance, paid vacations, performance bonuses, and career development programs. We believe in rewarding dedication and hard work with opportunities for advancement and recognition.

Team Building Activities

We organize frequent team-building events to foster collaboration and a positive work atmosphere. These events include outdoor adventures, creative workshops, and friendly competitions designed to strengthen bonds between colleagues.

Teamwork is at the heart of what we do, and our events encourage trust, communication, and mutual support. Whether it's an escape room challenge, a company retreat, or a casual get-together, we ensure that our employees have fun while building meaningful connections.

Career Growth Opportunities

Advance your career within our organization and take on new challenges as you develop your skills. We offer mentorship programs, leadership training, and opportunities to transition into higher roles based on performance and passion.

Many of our team members have started in entry-level positions and grown into managerial roles. We believe in promoting from within and providing guidance to those who seek continuous improvement in their careers.
