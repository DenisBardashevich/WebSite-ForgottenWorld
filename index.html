Welcome to Forgotten World, a unique quest-performance designed to immerse you in unforgettable experiences.

Forgotten World invites you to step into a world where reality and imagination merge. Our carefully crafted quests take you on journeys through mysterious landscapes, challenging puzzles, and immersive storytelling.

At Forgotten World, we believe in the power of live experiences to connect people, spark creativity, and build lasting memories. Every quest is an opportunity to explore, think critically, and work as a team to uncover secrets and solve mysteries.

Our quests are designed with the intention of blurring the lines between entertainment and art, offering an experience that transcends traditional boundaries and brings participants into a narrative like never before.

Whether you're exploring with friends or seeking a new challenge, Forgotten World promises an adventure that will leave you with more than just memories – it will inspire you to think, feel, and experience the world in new ways.

Our Quests

The Lost Temple Quest

Venture into the ancient jungle to discover a lost temple, solve puzzles, and unlock secrets hidden for centuries. A thrilling adventure awaits you as you uncover the mysteries of this forgotten world.

Experience the challenge of navigating treacherous terrain, decoding ancient scripts, and uncovering the temple’s hidden treasures. This quest will test your wit and bravery at every turn.

Secrets of the Forest

Unravel the hidden secrets of an enchanted forest. Follow the trail of ancient knowledge, solve cryptic puzzles, and confront the magical forces that protect the forest’s mysteries.

This immersive quest combines nature, mystery, and magic. Get lost in a world where every tree, every stone, holds a story waiting to be uncovered.

The Haunted Manor

Dare to enter the Haunted Manor, where the spirits of the past still linger. Solve the eerie puzzles that hold the key to unlocking the mystery of the manor’s dark history.

Explore a mansion filled with secrets, traps, and paranormal activity. Can you escape before the clock runs out?

The Alien Encounter

Prepare yourself for an intergalactic adventure as you try to communicate with extraterrestrial beings. Solve alien puzzles, navigate otherworldly landscapes, and uncover the truth behind the alien encounter.

This quest will challenge your thinking and push you to question everything you thought you knew about the universe.
